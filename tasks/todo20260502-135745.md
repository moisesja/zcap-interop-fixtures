### Discovery during validation

First re-run after the runner + fixture changes surfaced a third
upstream `zcap-dotnet` bug: every concrete `Caveat` subclass emits
duplicate `Type` and `type` JSON keys because
`[JsonPropertyName("type")]` on the abstract base property does not
propagate to derived `override` declarations on .NET 10 STJ. Filed as
`moisesja/zcap-dotnet#45`. Upstream landed the per-override attribute
fix in `e2a44e9` (PR #46) the same session.
